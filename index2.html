About Us

Christ Hospital, a unit of International Mission Hospitals across the world managed by Christ Hospital Trust
is a 150 bedded super multi specialty hospital located in the peaceful environment of Madhapar village, Rajkot.

The entire Specialist Team follows a Common Plan, so that no time is lost in getting expert advice for the simplest clinical problems or for the most complex disorders.

  • Highly qualified, skilled & experienced doctors.
  • 24 Hours round the clock Intensevist.
  • Choices of rooms depending of the affordability of the patient (General, Semi Special, AC Special, Deluxe, Suite room)

Christ Hospital Rajkot is a unit of International Mission Hospitals across the world. It is Managed by Christ Hospital Trust. Christ Hospital Trust is a voluntary, charitable, Catholic Christian Organization of the Diocese of Rajkot working for health care, health education and research.
