    About Us

    The early beginnings of Maharshi Atreya Health Centre can be traced back to 1932, when Dr. Chandulal Pandya started his Ayurvedic practice in Undera near Vadodara. His son Dr. Dinesh Pandya joined the practice in 1974. The present Maharshi Atreya Health Centre took shape in December 2000 as a full-fledged ayurvedic treatment facility.

    MAHC IS AN AYURVEDIC HEALTH CENTRE

    THE AYURVEDIC WAY

    Understanding these principles of Ayurved and living life according to them are the secrets to a long and disease-free life. The three doshas – Vata, Pitta and Kapha – are the fundamental forces in our body that determine the harmony and equilibrium in the body. If the balance between these forces is maintained, good health is maintained; if the balance is disturbed, unfavourable conditions are triggered in the body and this invites disease.

    Everything that we do in our daily life and everything that surrounds us in the world affect these three doshas in varying degrees. The effects can be in terms of the quantity of the doshas, the quality of the doshas and the ‘place’ of the doshas. When the balance is disturbed the body normally compensates for the changes automatically.
